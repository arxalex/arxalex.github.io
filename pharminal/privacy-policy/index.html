Privacy Policy for Pharminal

Effective Date: 01-Mar-2025

1. Introduction

Welcome to Pharminal. Your privacy is important to us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, store, and protect your information when you use our app.

2. Information We Collect

We do not collect personally identifiable information. However, we may store data necessary for app functionality, including:

  • Medication Data: Name, count, expiration date, and other pill-related details.
  • Favorites: Pills marked as favorites are stored locally.
  • Cloud Sync (if enabled): Data may be stored in iCloud for cross-device access.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your data solely to:

  • Help you organize and track your medications.
  • Sync data across devices via iCloud (if enabled).

  • All data is stored locally on your device.

  • We do not share, sell, or distribute your data to third parties.

5. Third-Party Services

  • The app may integrate with CloudKit (Apple's iCloud service) to sync your data securely.
  • We do not use third-party analytics or ads.

6. Your Choices & Control

  • You can delete any stored data at any time.
  • If using iCloud, disabling iCloud for Pharminal will stop syncing your data across devices.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We encourage you to review it periodically.

8. Contact Us
